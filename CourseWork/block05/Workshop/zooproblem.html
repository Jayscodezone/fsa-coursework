Bears

Bears are large carnivores and can be found all over the world. They like to sleep in dens and caves when it is time to hibernate. Bears like to live in environments such as mountains, tundras, and forests.

  • Ollie
  • Mona

Ollie

He is black bear who loves to snuggle and an expert in climbing trees. He loves to eat fish and swimming to catch them. Ollie is one of the largest male bears. He weights 450 lbs and stands 6 feet tall. Ollie can run up to a speed of 35 miles per hours, this comes in handy when he has to look for and smell food. Unfortunately , Ollie is a little shy of people.

Mona

She is a brown bear who loves to play fetch with Ollie. She loves to eat fruits and nuts. Mona is one of the smallest female bears. She weights 150 lbs and stands 5 feet tall. Mona can run up to a speed of 25 miles per hours, this comes in handy when she has to look for and smell food. Mona is a very friendly, protective and loves to play with people.

Giraffes

Giraffes are the tallest living animals in the world. They can grow up to 34 feet tall. They have very long necks with wide shoulders, and powerful legs. Giraffes are solitary animals who are naturally from the continent of Africa. They love to play, eat and sleep. Giraffes are considered to be the world's largest herbivores which means they are animals that have a plant based diets.

  • Frankie
  • Coconut

Frankie

Frankie is a Masai giraffe and he is from southern Kenya. Masai giraffes are much darker than other species of giraffes. Frankie is very active during the day and before nightfall. Frankie has large brown patches on their overcoat with a vine leaf and jagged shapes. Frankie likes to wander and run before meal times. He loves to eat leaves and drink gallons of water. Frankie likes to meet new people.

Coconut

Coconut is a Reticulated giraffe and he is from southern Kenya. Reticulated giraffes are considered the most stunning species among the giraffes. Coconut's overcoat is light brown with geometrical shapes. She is very tall and stands at 6 meets tall. Coconut loves to eat seed pods and fruits. Coconut is scared to see people as there her species type is very scarce.

Lions

Lions are the largest predators in the animal kingdom. They can grow up to 18 feet tall. Lions are solitary animals who are naturally from the continent of Africa. Lions love to play, eat and sleep. Lions are considered to be the world's largest herbivores which means they are animals that have a plant based diets.

Monkeys

Monkeys are the smallest animals in the animal kingdom. They can grow up to 1.5 feet tall. Monkeys are very interesting animals that can be friendly at times or can be very aggressive. Monkeys love to play, eat and sleep. Monkeys are also known as primates.

  • Cookie
  • Earl
  • Banana Pudding

Cookie

Cookie is a medium sized monkey. She is the oldest monkey in the animal kingdom out of the three monkeys. She is very maternal and loves to eat berries.

Earl

Earl is a large monkey and bigger than Cookie. He is the second oldest monkey in the zoo. He is a very intelligent animal. He likes to play dress up in his spare time. Earl loves to eat banana.

Banana Pudding

Banana Pudding is a small monkey. She is the youngest monkey in the animal kingdom out of the three monkeys. She is a very intelligent animal. She likes to play dress up in her spare time. Banana Pudding loves to eat peanuts.
